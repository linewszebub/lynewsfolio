UX/UI Design

Project: Tomorrow Smile

Published on May 26, 2017 by uixgeek on UX design


In 2020, our team embarked on a crucial project focused on web design with a central theme: addressing the global impact of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Recognizing the urgency and significance of the situation, we set out to create a website that not only served as an informative resource but also provided a user-friendly platform for individuals seeking reliable information about the virus. The design aimed to balance visual appeal with clear, concise content to ensure accessibility for a diverse audience. Our web design project featured an intuitive navigation system, enabling users to easily access sections dedicated to pandemic-related information, safety guidelines, and updates from reputable health organizations. The color scheme and imagery were thoughtfully selected to convey a sense of solidarity and empathy, recognizing the emotional strain the pandemic imposed on individuals worldwide. Responsiveness and adaptability were prioritized to cater to users across various devices, ensuring seamless access to critical information regardless of the platform. To enhance user engagement, we incorporated interactive elements such as data visualizations, infographics, and a dynamic FAQ section that evolved based on emerging questions from the community. The project aimed not only to disseminate essential information but also to foster a sense of community by providing a platform for shared experiences, resources, and support. In essence, our web design project of 2020 stood as a testament to the power of design in facilitating information dissemination and fostering a sense of unity during challenging times.
